# Praying With Trust

Prayer is not a performance.
It’s a relationship.

Trust grows when we pray honestly—without fear or pressure.

God values sincerity over perfection.

## What We Can Learn

Prayer builds trust.
God welcomes honest hearts.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“Let us therefore come boldly to the throne of grace.” — Hebrews 4:16

## Talk About It

- What makes prayer feel difficult sometimes?
- How can honesty deepen prayer?
- What does trusting God in prayer look like?
