# Learning to Wait

Waiting is one of the hardest parts of faith.

I used to think that if God cared, He would answer immediately. When prayers went unanswered, doubt started to creep in.

But over time, I realized something important:
God’s silence is not absence.

While I waited, God shaped my character.
While I waited, He strengthened my faith.
While I waited, He prepared answers I couldn’t yet see.

Waiting taught me how to trust, not just hope.

## What We Can Learn

God’s timing is intentional.
Delay is not denial.
Faith grows in the waiting.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“The LORD is good to those who wait for Him.” — Lamentations 3:25

## Talk About It

- What makes waiting difficult for you?
- How can waiting strengthen faith?
- What does trusting God look like when answers don’t come quickly?
