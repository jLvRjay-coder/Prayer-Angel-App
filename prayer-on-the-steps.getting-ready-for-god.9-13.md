# Getting Ready for God

📖 *Faith doesn’t always shout.*  
🕊️ *Sometimes it sits quietly… with an open Bible.*

---

## 🪜 A Father’s Peace

My dad was an ordained minister who loved God deeply.  
His faith wasn’t something he talked about — it was how he lived.

When I was about twelve years old, my dad became very sick.  
The diagnosis was serious.

I was scared.

But my dad wasn’t.

---

## 📖 Late Nights on the Steps

Late at night, I would wake up and see him sitting in his old lounge chair, reading his Bible.

No television.  
No distractions.  
Just Scripture… and peace.

When I asked him what he was doing, his answer never changed:

> *“I’m getting ready for God.”*

---

## 🙏 A Child’s Prayer

I prayed hard for my dad to be healed.  
I believed God could do it.  
I believed He would.

But as a child, when my dad didn’t get better, it felt like my prayer had gone unanswered.

I didn’t understand then what I know now.

---

## 🕯️ Understanding God’s Answer

God did heal my dad — completely.

Not by keeping him on earth,  
but by welcoming him into Heaven.

At the age of 44, on Thanksgiving Day, God called my dad home.

That was the day I learned a truth that changed me forever:

✨ **God’s answers are always loving — even when they hurt.**

---

## 🌿 What This Teaches Us

God answers prayer in different ways:

✔️ Yes  
✋ No  
⏳ Wait  

But He never ignores His children.

Faith means trusting God —  
even when the road is hard,  
even when the answer isn’t what we hoped for,  
even when we don’t understand yet.
