# God Is Still Close

Sometimes God feels quiet.

I wondered if He was still near when I couldn’t feel Him.

Then I learned something important:
God doesn’t leave just because things get hard.

Even when I feel lonely,
Even when I feel scared,
Even when I feel unsure—

God is still close.

## What We Can Learn

God never leaves us.
He is close even when we don’t feel Him.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“The LORD is near to all who call upon Him.” — Psalm 145:18

## Talk About It

- Have you ever felt like God was quiet?
- How can you remind yourself God is close?
- What helps you feel near to God?
