# God Is Still Close

There are moments when faith feels silent.

No answers.
No signs.
Just quiet.

But silence does not mean distance.

God remains near in every season—especially the hard ones.

Faith means trusting His presence even when emotions don’t confirm it.

## What We Can Learn

God’s nearness is constant.
Feelings change; God does not.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“Draw near to God and He will draw near to you.” — James 4:8

## Talk About It

- What makes God feel distant sometimes?
- How can you stay connected during quiet seasons?
- What helps you trust God’s nearness?
