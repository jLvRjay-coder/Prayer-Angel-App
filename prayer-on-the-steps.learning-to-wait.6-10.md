# Learning to Wait

Waiting is hard.

When I was little, I wanted God to answer my prayers right away. When He didn’t, I thought maybe He didn’t hear me.

One day I asked my mom, “Why does God take so long?”

She smiled and said, “Sometimes waiting is part of how God helps us grow.”

I didn’t like that answer. But over time, I started to notice something.

While I waited, God was teaching me patience.
While I waited, He was teaching me trust.
While I waited, He was still close.

God wasn’t ignoring me.
He was preparing me.

## What We Can Learn

God’s timing is always right.
Waiting does not mean no.
Waiting means God is still working.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“Wait on the LORD; be of good courage, and He shall strengthen your heart.” — Psalm 27:14

## Talk About It

- Is there something you are waiting on right now?
- How can you trust God while you wait?
- What can waiting teach us?
