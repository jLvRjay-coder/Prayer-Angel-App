# 📖 God Hears Your Prayers

*A true story of childlike faith and God’s faithfulness.*

## Growing Up With a Heart for God

When I was little, something inside me was drawn to God. I wanted to know who Jesus is and why church matters. No one in my home went to church—but I felt a pull, so I started going on my own.

I joined Bible School and everything my little church offered. I listened to the songs, learned the lessons, and tried to remember every Scripture. But I had a quiet wish deep inside me:

**I wanted my family to know God too.**

When I was nine years old, I gave my heart to Jesus. It was real. It changed me. And my prayer for my family grew even stronger.

## A Little Prayer Closet

I made a “prayer closet”—a small, hidden space where I could kneel down and talk to God. If that old home were still standing, I could walk you right to the exact spot.

In that little place, I prayed the same thing again and again:

**“Lord, please lead Mom and Dad to church… and let them take me.  
And God… please call my dad to preach.”**

My parents were good, loving, hardworking people. But I wanted them to follow Jesus and love His Word the way I did.

## And Then God Moved

When I was ten, I sat in church and watched something I will never forget.

My dad was **ordained as a Baptist minister.**

The prayer I whispered in secret… God answered in public.

That day, my parents walked into church like normal people—  
and we walked out knowing God had heard me all along.

## A Final Encouragement

Whether you are **6 or 60**, God still hears you.

Keep praying. Keep trusting. Keep believing.  
God is faithful—even when we can’t see what He’s doing yet.
