# David and Goliath (Ages 6–10)

David was a shepherd boy.
He took care of sheep and trusted God.

One day, a giant named Goliath scared God’s people.
No one wanted to fight him.

David knew God was bigger than the giant.
He said he came **in the name of the Lord**.

David used a sling and one stone.
God helped David win the battle.

The giant fell — not because David was strong,
but because God was faithful.

### What This Teaches
- God is stronger than anything we fear.
- God helps us when we trust Him.
- You don’t have to be big to be brave.

## Think About It
1) What makes you feel scared sometimes?
2) How can you trust God when you’re afraid?
3) What can you pray about today?
