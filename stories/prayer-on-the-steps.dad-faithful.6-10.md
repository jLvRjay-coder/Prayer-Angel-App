# 📖 Daddy Was Getting Ready for God (6–10)

## A gentle story about faith, prayer, and God’s perfect “yes”

When I was **12 years old**, I found out my dad was very sick.  
The doctors said it was **serious**, and it scared me.

I didn’t understand everything.  
I just knew it was **bad**.

Dad told me something that stayed in my heart:

**“God is going to heal me.”**

As a kid, I believed him.  
I thought healing meant dad would get better and stay here with us.

### Late Nights in the Lounge Chair

Some nights I would wake up in the **middle of the night**…  
and I would see my dad sitting in his **old lounge chair**.

He was reading his Bible.

I asked him, “Daddy, what are you doing?”

And every time his answer was the same:

**“I’m getting ready for God.”**

### God’s Answer Looked Different

I prayed and prayed for my dad to be healed.

But the healing I wanted… didn’t happen the way I expected.

Later in life, I learned something important:

Sometimes God heals by bringing someone **home to Heaven**.

### A Hope That Still Holds

Even when I didn’t understand, God was still good.  
And my dad’s faith was real.

God didn’t ignore my prayers.

God answered… **in His way**… at the right time.
