# The Death of King Saul (Ages 6–10)

King Saul stopped listening to God.
Over time, his heart became hard.

David had chances to hurt Saul.
But David chose not to.
He trusted God to do what was right.

Later, King Saul died in battle.
David did not celebrate.
He was sad and showed respect.

David waited for God’s timing.
God kept His promise.

### What This Teaches
- We should not take revenge.
- God keeps His promises.
- Doing what is right matters, even when it’s hard.

## Think About It
1) How should we treat people who hurt us?
2) What does it mean to wait for God?
3) Who can you forgive today?
