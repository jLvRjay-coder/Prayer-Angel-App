# David in King Saul’s Court (Ages 6–10)

After David helped defeat Goliath, King Saul asked David to serve him.

David played music for Saul when Saul felt troubled.
God used David to bring peace.

But when people praised David, King Saul became jealous.
Instead of trusting God, Saul became angry.

David stayed kind and humble.
He did not fight back.
He trusted God to take care of him.

### What This Teaches
- God sees our hearts.
- Serving others matters.
- Jealousy hurts, but kindness brings peace.

## Think About It
1) How can you serve others with a good heart?
2) What should you do when someone is mean to you?
3) Who can you pray for today?
