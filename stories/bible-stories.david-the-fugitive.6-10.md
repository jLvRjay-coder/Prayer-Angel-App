# David the Fugitive (Ages 6–10)

King Saul became very angry with David.
David had done nothing wrong.

David had to run and hide.
He lived in caves and faraway places.

David felt scared sometimes,
but God stayed with him.

Other people came to David.
They were hurting and afraid too.

David welcomed them.
God was teaching David how to lead with kindness.

### What This Teaches
- God stays close when life feels scary.
- God brings helpers when we feel alone.
- Hard times help us grow stronger.

## Think About It
1) When have you felt scared or alone?
2) Who can you pray for today?
3) How can you trust God when things feel hard?
