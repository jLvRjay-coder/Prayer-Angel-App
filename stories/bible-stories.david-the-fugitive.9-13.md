# David the Fugitive (Ages 9–13)

David was forced to run for his life.
Not because he had done wrong — but because King Saul refused to trust God.

David hid in caves and wilderness places.
He felt hunted, misunderstood, and tired.

Yet God was still working.

Men began to gather around David.
They were distressed, in debt, and discouraged.
God brought them together for a purpose.

David did not lead from a throne.
He learned to lead with patience, mercy, and faith.

The cave became a classroom.
God was preparing David for what was coming next.

### What This Teaches
- God develops leaders before He promotes them.
- Hard seasons shape character.
- God can use broken people for His purpose.

## Reflection (9–13)
1) What kind of pressure are you under right now?
2) How might God be using this season to grow you?
3) Who can you encourage this week?
