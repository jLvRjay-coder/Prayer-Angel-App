# David Anointed King (Ages 6–10)

God had a plan for David.
When David was young, God chose him to be king.

David had to wait a long time.
He trusted God while he waited.

After King Saul died, God kept His promise.
David became king, just as God said he would.

David thanked God and led the people with joy.

### What This Teaches
- God always keeps His promises.
- Waiting can be hard, but God is worth trusting.
- God’s timing is always right.

## Think About It
1) Have you ever had to wait for something important?
2) How can you trust God while you wait?
3) What is one way you can thank God today?
