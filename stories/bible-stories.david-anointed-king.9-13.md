# David Anointed King (Ages 9–13)

David was anointed as king — twice.

First, when he was a young shepherd.
Later, after years of waiting, hardship, and trusting God.

David never forced his future.
He chose obedience over control and patience over revenge.

When David finally became king, it wasn’t sudden.
It was the result of faithfulness in hidden seasons.

God’s promises are never late.
They are fulfilled at the right time.

### What This Teaches
- God prepares leaders before He promotes them.
- Waiting builds character.
- Obedience matters more than timing.

## Reflection (9–13)
1) What promise from God encourages you right now?
2) How are you responding to waiting?
3) What might God be shaping in you during this season?
