# The Death of King Saul (Ages 9–13)

Saul’s story is a warning.
Not because God failed — but because Saul stopped trusting God.

David had opportunities to take revenge.
He refused.
David understood that God alone removes and establishes leaders.

When Saul died in battle, David did not celebrate.
He mourned.

David trusted God’s justice instead of acting in anger.
Because of that obedience, God’s promise continued forward.

### What This Teaches
- Revenge hardens the heart.
- God is just, even when life feels unfair.
- Waiting on God builds integrity.

## Reflection (9–13)
1) Why is revenge tempting when someone hurts us?
2) What does it look like to trust God with justice?
3) Where might God be asking you to wait?
