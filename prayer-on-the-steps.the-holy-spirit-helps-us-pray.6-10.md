# The Helper Who Prays With Us

## When Words Are Hard to Find

Sometimes I wanted to pray, but I didn’t know what to say.

I knew God loved me.  
I knew He listened.  
But the words wouldn’t come.

My mama told me something I never forgot:

“You’re not praying alone.”

---

## God Gave Us a Helper

God knew we would have days when praying feels hard.

So He gave us the Holy Spirit.

The Holy Spirit helps us talk to God — even when we don’t know what to say.

When we feel sad… He helps.  
When we feel scared… He helps.  
When we feel quiet inside… He helps.

---

## God Still Hears You

Even when your prayer feels small,  
even when your prayer feels messy,  
God still hears.

The Holy Spirit takes what’s in your heart and helps bring it to God.

You are never alone when you pray.

---

## Scripture to Read (KJV)

> “Likewise the Spirit also helpeth our infirmities: for we know not what we should pray for as we ought.”
> **Romans 8:26**

---

## Simple Rhythm

**Feel → Trust → Pray**
