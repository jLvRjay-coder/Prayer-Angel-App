# When God Answers in His Time

## Adult — Peace in the Delay

There are seasons where you pray faithfully and still feel stuck.

You do what you know is right.  
You ask God for direction.  
You wait.  
And the answer doesn’t arrive on your timeline.

That can stir frustration, fear, and even doubt.

But Scripture teaches a steady truth: God works in seasons—on purpose.

We often measure God’s care by speed.  
But God measures by formation.

The delay is not always denial.  
Sometimes the wait is where God is building patience, humility, endurance, and deeper trust.  
Sometimes He is protecting you from what you can’t see yet.  
Sometimes He is aligning people, timing, and circumstances that are beyond your reach.

The church steps are a picture of that spiritual space:  
a pause between the noise of the world and the stillness of worship.

Waiting is not wasted when it’s surrendered.

If you’re in a season of delay, keep praying.  
Keep obeying.  
Keep showing up.

God is not late.  
He is faithful.  
And He answers in His time.
