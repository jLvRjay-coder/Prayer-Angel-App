# Praying With Trust

Prayer isn’t about saying perfect words.

It’s about trusting God with your heart.

I learned that God listens, even when my prayers are small.

He hears whispers.
He hears tears.
He hears hope.

## What We Can Learn

God listens to every prayer.
We can trust Him with everything.

## Scripture (NKJV)

“The LORD hears when I call to Him.” — Psalm 4:3

## Talk About It

- What do you like to pray about?
- How can you trust God when you pray?
- Why is prayer important?
