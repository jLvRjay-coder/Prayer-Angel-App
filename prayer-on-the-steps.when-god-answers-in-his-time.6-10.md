# When God Answers in His Time

## Ages 6–10 — Waiting on the Steps

Some days, the church steps were loud with laughter.  
Other days, they were quiet.

One Sunday, a little girl sat on the steps with her hands folded tight.  
She had prayed all week.

She prayed for her dad.  
She prayed for her friend.  
She prayed for something she wanted God to fix right away.

But nothing had changed.

She looked at the steps and wondered,  
“Did God hear me?”

The Bible tells us that God always hears His children.  
But sometimes, God’s answer is not right away  
(Psalm 27:14, KJV).

Waiting does not mean God forgot.  
Waiting means God is still working.

Just like seeds take time to grow,  
God’s answers grow in the right moment.

So the little girl stayed on the steps.  
She prayed again.  
And she trusted God.

**Simple truth for kids (6–10):**  
God hears every prayer. Sometimes His answer takes time, but it is always loving.
