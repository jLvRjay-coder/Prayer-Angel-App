# Learning to Trust God Daily

## Ages 6–10 — One Step at a Time

Every morning, the church steps felt different.

Some days they were warm from the sun.  
Some days they were cold and quiet.

A little boy liked to sit on the steps before going inside. He talked to God there. Not big prayers — just honest ones.

“Help me today.”  
“Stay with me.”  
“Please don’t leave.”

The Bible says we can trust God every day, not just when things feel scary  
(Proverbs 3:5–6, KJV).

Trusting God doesn’t mean knowing everything will be easy.  
It means knowing God will walk with us.

The boy stood up, took a deep breath, and went inside.

One step at a time.  
One day at a time.  
God was still with him.

### Simple truth for kids (6–10)
You can trust God every day, even when you don’t know what will happen.
