# God Answered My Prayer

Sometimes God answers our prayers in ways we don’t expect — but He always answers with love.

## Watching My Dad Trust God

My dad was an ordained minister who loved God very much. He was faithful to his calling and trusted the Lord with his whole heart.

When I was about twelve years old, my dad became very sick. The doctors said he had a serious illness called melanoma. I didn’t fully understand what that meant — I only knew that it was bad, and that I was scared.

But my dad was not afraid.

He told me again and again that God was with him and that everything would be okay.

## Late-Night Prayers

Many nights, I would wake up and find my dad sitting in his old lounge chair, reading his Bible.

When I asked him what he was doing, he always answered the same way:

> “I’m getting ready for God.”

I prayed hard for my dad to be healed. I asked God to make him better and let him stay with us.

## God’s Answer

As a child, I didn’t think my prayer was answered — because my dad did not get better here on earth.

But later, I learned something very important.

God *did* heal my dad.

God welcomed him into heaven, where there is no sickness, no pain, and no sadness. My dad received a brand-new body and now lives forever with Jesus.

## What I Learned

God always hears our prayers.  
Sometimes He says **yes**.  
Sometimes He says **wait**.  
And sometimes He answers in a bigger, better way than we can understand.

### Scripture (NKJV)

> “The LORD is near to those who have a broken heart.”  
> — Psalm 34:18

> “Let not your heart be troubled… I go to prepare a place for you.”  
> — John 14:1–2
